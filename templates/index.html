Hybrid Fake News Detection System

A powerful real-time News Verification System using a Hybrid Model: Google Fact Check API + Artificial Intelligence + Source Credibility Analysis.

Check any news — including breaking news — instantly. If Google Fact Check results are available, they are used. Else our AI + credibility engine predicts whether the news is Likely Real, Likely Fake, or Uncertain.
